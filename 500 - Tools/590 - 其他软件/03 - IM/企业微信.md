### 二、下载安装

```shell
wget http://packages.deepin.com/deepin/pool/non-free/d/deepin.com.weixin.work/deepin.com.weixin.work_2.8.10.2010deepin0_i386.deb

sudo dpkg -i deepin.com.weixin.work_2.8.10.2010deepin0_i386.deb
```
